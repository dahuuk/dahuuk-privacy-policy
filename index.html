Welcome to Dahuuk ("we," "our," or "us"). We are strongly committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. By downloading, installing, registering, or using the Dahuuk mobile application or website (collectively referred to as the "Platform"), you explicitly consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any terms outlined herein, please terminate your access and uninstall the application immediately.

1. About Dahuuk and How It Works

Dahuuk is an online on-demand digital service marketplace platform. It acts as a reliable bridge connecting service seekers (Customers) with verified professional service providers (Vendors). Dahuuk does not directly provide the services listed on the platform; it serves strictly as a transparent, secure, and robust digital medium to facilitate communications, bookings, and financial transactions between both parties.

2. Information We Collect

To deliver a secure, functional, and high-quality marketplace environment, we collect several categories of information depending on whether you register as a Customer or a Vendor:

A. Personal Identity & Profile Data:

  • For Customers: Full name, an active mobile phone number (for OTP verification), and an email address (for account login, transaction receipts, and official notifications).
  • For Vendors: Full name, mobile phone number, National Identity Card (NID) or official legal documents or certificates (for mandatory vendor verification), permanent and present addresses, work experience, portfolio details, and service rate sheets.

B. Device Permissions & Geographical Data (Critical Play Console Support):

  • Location Permission: Dahuuk requires location data to match Customers with nearby active Vendors, compute accurate service distance matrices, and ensure reliable order deployment. This data is collected strictly while the application is open and running in the foreground.
  • Camera & Gallery Permission: We require access to your device’s camera and photo gallery only when you explicitly choose to upload NID images, a profile picture (for both users) or upload work portfolio images (for Vendors). The app never accesses your files or camera without your direct active command.

C. Financial and Payment Information:

Dahuuk supports a hybrid transaction ecosystem to facilitate seamless payments. In the initial phase, Cash on Delivery (COD) or direct cash payments upon service completion is the primary transaction method. Additionally, the platform is structured to support integrated digital payments, including authorized third-party payment gateways (SSLCommerz) and official Mobile Financial Services (MFS) merchant integrations such as bKash and Nagad for future deployments.

Dahuuk does not directly store, collect, or process your credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or MFS account PINs on its own servers. For all payment structures, we only maintain tokenized transactional histories, including order amounts, booking IDs, cash confirmation statuses, and vendor commission or withdrawal logs for operational audit and legal bookkeeping purposes.

3. Third-Party Services, Analytics, and Infrastructure

To optimize performance, track stability, and maintain application health, we integrate industry-standard third-party developer tools:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: In the event of an unexpected application crash or technical error, automated diagnostic logs (including device model, OS version, and crash stack traces) are collected to help our developers fix bugs promptly.
  • Firebase & Google Analytics: We track aggregated, anonymous user behavior data (such as frequently visited categories or button clicks) to understand user engagement and continuously improve the app's User Experience (UX).
  • Push Notifications: We use device notification services to send you crucial operational alerts, including order confirmations, vendor assignment updates, and verification status alerts. You can opt out of these at any time via your device settings.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Our website platform may utilize standard browser cookies to maintain active login sessions, preserve user preferences, and optimize web page loading speeds.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain a strict anti-spam policy. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third-party marketers for commercial purposes. Data sharing is strictly confined to the following structural use cases:

  • Between Customer and Vendor: Once an order is officially confirmed, the platform securely shares standard contact details (Name and Phone Number) and the physical service address between the assigned Vendor and Customer solely to enable the physical execution and localized completion of the service.
  • Legal and Regulatory Mandates: We reserve the right to disclose personal data if required by enforceable legal processes, court orders, or statutory mandates under the prevailing digital laws of the Government of Bangladesh.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion Policy (Google Play Compliant)

  • Data Retention: We retain your personal information only as long as your platform account remains active or as strictly required to fulfill legitimate business reporting, financial auditing, and legal dispute resolution frameworks.
  • Account Deletion: In compliance with modern global data privacy rules and Google Play Data Deletion mandates, Dahuuk provides all users with complete autonomy over their data. Users can permanently request the deletion of their entire account and all associated personal history directly through the application's settings panel or via a dedicated external web form. Upon receiving a validated request, your data will be permanently scrubbed from our active production databases within a standardized legal window, except where specific financial transaction logs must be preserved by national law.

6. Children's Privacy

The Dahuuk marketplace is built exclusively for individuals who are at least 18 years of age or older and are legally capable of executing binding contracts. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from minors under the age of 18. If we discover that an account has been created by a minor using fraudulent details, we will immediately deactivate the account and permanently purge their data from our systems.

Dahuuk currently operates within the geographical borders of Bangladesh, and all user records are stored on secure cloud databases. In the future, if the platform scales or transitions to international data distribution centers, your data will continue to be protected under robust, high-tier encryption standards.
